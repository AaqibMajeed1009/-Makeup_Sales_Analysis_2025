# 🧑‍💻 What I Did in This Project

In this project, I performed a comprehensive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a makeup sales dataset for the year 2025 with the aim of extracting meaningful insights and understanding overall business performance.

I began by importing the dataset and conducting an initial inspection to understand its structure, data types, and key variables. This included identifying important columns such as product type, brand, units sold, revenue, sales channel, and payment method.

🔹 Data Cleaning & Preprocessing

A significant part of the project involved preparing the dataset for analysis. I handled missing values, removed duplicate records, and corrected inconsistencies in the data. This ensured that the dataset was clean, reliable, and ready for further analysis.

🔹 Data Manipulation & Transformation

Using Pandas and NumPy, I performed data transformations and aggregations. I grouped data based on different features such as product categories, sales channels, and brands to compute metrics like total revenue, average order value, and units sold.

🔹 Exploratory Data Analysis

I explored the dataset to identify patterns and trends, including:

Sales performance across different product types
Comparison of online vs retail sales channels
Revenue contribution by various payment methods
Brand-wise performance across different platforms
Monthly variations in sales to identify trends and seasonality
🔹 Data Visualization

To better understand and communicate insights, I created multiple visualizations using Matplotlib and Seaborn:

Bar charts to compare sales across product categories and brands
Line graphs to analyze trends over time
Heatmaps to examine correlations between numerical variables
Scatter plots to study relationships between units sold and revenue

These visualizations helped in simplifying complex data and making patterns more interpretable.

🔹 Insight Generation

Based on the analysis, I derived key business insights such as:

Identification of top-performing product categories
Understanding which sales channels generate higher revenue
Observing customer preferences in payment methods
Detecting trends and fluctuations in monthly sales
